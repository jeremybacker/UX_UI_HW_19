My Skills

User Research

The discovery, research, and empathy phases are paramount to a successful product. I thoroughly enjoy learning as much as possible about the users’ needs and frustrations before designing, in an effort to make products that are not only beautiful but serve their purpose...well...beautifully.

User Interface Design

Not just a shiny coat of paint on all of the hard work the UX team has executed! UI is the face of the product, and often can be the determining factor of that product’s success. I apply my extensive knowledge in the visual design realm to really make the products sing.

Brand Design

I have over 10 years experience working on branding projects both big and small. Understanding a company’s position, culture, and impact simply by seeing their brand in action can be very powerful. I relish the opportunity to help brands find their voice and express it in a way only they could.
